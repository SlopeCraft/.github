# SlopeCraft Community Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and technically constructive community.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- discussing ideas and code in good faith;
- giving specific, actionable, and respectful feedback;
- acknowledging different experience levels and perspectives;
- respecting project scope, contributor time, and licensing constraints;
- correcting mistakes openly and helping others learn.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks;
- sexualized language or unwanted attention;
- deliberate disruption, trolling, or repeated bad-faith argument;
- publishing another person's private information without permission;
- pressuring maintainers or contributors through entitlement or intimidation;
- using project spaces for spam, scams, or unrelated promotion.

## Scope

This code applies in repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, review comments, and other spaces where a person is representing the SlopeCraft community.

## Reporting

For immediate threats or platform abuse, use GitHub's reporting tools.

For project-community conduct concerns, contact an organization owner or the relevant repository maintainer through an available private channel. Do not publish sensitive personal information in a public issue.

Reports will be reviewed in good faith. Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation, or ban participants when necessary.

## Enforcement principles

Enforcement should be proportionate, documented where appropriate, and focused on protecting contributors and restoring productive collaboration. Retaliation against a person who reports a concern in good faith is not acceptable.

# SlopeCraft 社区行为准则

## 社区标准

我们致力于建设一个友善、尊重他人且有利于技术协作的社区。

积极行为包括：

- 以善意讨论方案和代码；
- 提供具体、可执行且尊重他人的反馈；
- 尊重不同经验水平和观点；
- 尊重项目范围、贡献者时间和许可证约束；
- 公开纠正错误并帮助他人学习。

不可接受的行为包括：

- 骚扰、歧视、威胁或人身攻击；
- 性暗示内容或不受欢迎的关注；
- 故意扰乱讨论、挑衅或反复进行恶意争论；
- 未经允许公开他人的私人信息；
- 以特权心态、施压或恐吓方式对待维护者和贡献者；
- 在项目空间发布垃圾信息、诈骗内容或无关推广。

## 适用范围

本准则适用于仓库、Issue、Pull Request、Discussion、审查评论，以及任何代表 SlopeCraft 社区参与的场合。

## 举报

如遇紧迫威胁或平台滥用，请使用 GitHub 的举报工具。

如需举报项目社区中的行为问题，请通过可用的私密渠道联系组织 Owner 或相关仓库维护者。不要在公开 Issue 中发布敏感个人信息。

维护者会善意审查举报，并可根据情况删除内容、发出警告、限制参与或封禁相关人员。

## 执行原则

处理措施应与问题严重程度相称，并在适当情况下留下记录，重点是保护贡献者并恢复有效协作。不得报复善意举报者。
