# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do not report security vulnerabilities through public issues, pull requests, discussions, or social media.

Use the affected repository's **Security** tab and choose **Report a vulnerability** when private vulnerability reporting is available.

If that option is unavailable, contact an organization owner or repository maintainer through an existing private channel. If you must request a private contact publicly, do not include technical details, proof-of-concept material, credentials, personal data, or other sensitive information.

Please include:

- the affected repository, version, tag, or commit;
- the vulnerability type and expected impact;
- reproduction steps or a minimal proof of concept;
- relevant configuration and environment details;
- known mitigations or workarounds;
- your preferred contact method and disclosure expectations.

## Scope

Security support generally applies to actively maintained SlopeCraft repositories and currently supported releases.

Archived repositories, experiments, vendored code, and upstream forks may receive best-effort handling or be redirected to the relevant upstream project. A repository-specific security policy takes precedence over this default policy.

## Response and disclosure

Maintainers will attempt to acknowledge a complete report, assess severity, coordinate a fix, and publish an advisory when appropriate. Response times depend on maintainer availability and the complexity of the issue.

Please allow a reasonable remediation period before public disclosure. We will coordinate credit with the reporter unless anonymity is requested.

## Good-faith research

Good-faith security research that avoids privacy violations, service disruption, data destruction, and unauthorized persistence is welcome. Do not access or modify data beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the issue.

# 安全策略

## 报告安全漏洞

不要通过公开 Issue、Pull Request、Discussion 或社交媒体报告安全漏洞。

如果受影响仓库的 **Security** 页面提供 **Report a vulnerability**，请使用该私密漏洞报告功能。

如果该功能不可用，请通过已有私密渠道联系组织 Owner 或仓库维护者。如必须公开请求私密联系方式，请勿附带技术细节、概念验证、凭据、个人数据或其他敏感信息。

报告中请尽量包含：

- 受影响的仓库、版本、标签或提交；
- 漏洞类型及预期影响；
- 重现步骤或最小概念验证；
- 相关配置和环境；
- 已知缓解措施或临时方案；
- 希望使用的联系方式和披露安排。

## 支持范围

安全支持通常覆盖仍在积极维护的 SlopeCraft 仓库和当前受支持版本。

已归档仓库、实验项目、内嵌第三方代码和上游分叉可能仅提供尽力支持，或转交相应上游项目。仓库自己的安全策略优先于本默认策略。

## 响应与披露

维护者会尽力确认完整报告、评估严重程度、协调修复，并在适当情况下发布安全公告。响应时间取决于维护者可用时间和问题复杂度。

公开披露前请预留合理的修复时间。除非报告者要求匿名，我们会协调署名方式。

## 善意研究

欢迎不侵犯隐私、不中断服务、不破坏数据且不建立未授权持久访问的善意安全研究。请勿访问或修改超出证明问题所必需的数据。
